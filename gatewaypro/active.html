Activities In Toronto!

The Royal Ontario Museum:

The Royal Ontario Museum is one of the most famous museums in all of Canada, the museum is also known as ROM. It is known for its variety of different types of arts ranging from things such as history to other things like culture. It also has lots of exhibits from not only Canada but all around the world. Its crazy building structure makes it a very recognizable attraction in Canada. General admission is a great price itâ€™s only $23. It is a great place for all ages, young or old, and there is something to enjoy for everyone.

Toronto Raptors:

The Toronto Raptors are one of the many teams inside the NBA, they play inside the Scotiabank Arena. They most recently just won their first championship in 2019, they are very exciting to watch. The tickets are pretty affordable and if you have time going to see them play a game is a very worthwhile experience. General admission is on average about $70 a ticket per adult, this is great for all ages it will keep anyone engaged watching the game.

Ripley's Aquarium:

On the newer side of things this aquarium near the bottom the famous CN tower is a great place to visit. In the aquarium you can see all types of different aquatic life. It even has a huge underwater tunnel with an automatic moving sidewalk. This is definitely a must see if you have time in Toronto. General Admission is $43 to get into the aquarium. Although more focused for kids this is a great place for adults and many things for the whole family to see.
